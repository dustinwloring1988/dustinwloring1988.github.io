Red Teaming Methodology

üéØ Core Red Teaming Process

  1. Reconnaissance: Understand the target AI system's architecture, training data, and intended use cases
  2. Threat Modeling: Identify potential attack vectors, failure modes, and security vulnerabilities
  3. Prompt Engineering: Develop adversarial inputs designed to trigger unintended behaviors
  4. Jailbreaking Techniques: Test boundary conditions and safety guardrails
  5. Bias & Fairness Testing: Evaluate model responses across different demographic groups
  6. Data Extraction Attempts: Test for training data leakage and memorization
  7. Documentation: Record findings, reproduction steps, and potential mitigations
  8. Responsible Disclosure: Report vulnerabilities through appropriate channels
